Over-the-Counter Payment Instructions

For Aesthetic Purposes Only

To complete your order using Over-the-Counter payment, please follow these steps:

  • Visit your nearest Xelor's Cafe branch or partner outlet.
  • Provide the cashier with your Order ID or Invoice Number.
  • Pay the amount due as displayed in your order summary.
  • Retain the receipt as proof of payment.

Once your payment is confirmed, your order will be processed immediately.
